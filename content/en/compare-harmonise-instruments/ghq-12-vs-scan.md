## GHQ 12 vs SCAN

Psychologists frequently find themselves comparing GHQ 12 and SCAN, two instruments utilized for identifying psychological distress. The GHQ 12 questionnaire encapsulates 12 questions related to emotional well-being, while SCAN (Schedules for Clinical Assessment in Neuropsychiatry) offers a broader spectrum with over 1800 potential items.
